Who We Are

Gamma Nu Eta is the national Information Technology Honor Society. Our role in the ITWS department is to organize and host academic and professional development related events. The Gamma Nu Eta leadership team also works closely with the ITWS department to assist in curriculum decisions and admissions events. Members of Gamma Nu Eta will be involved in communicating directly with company recruiters for the Company Expose, to helping ITWS students prepare for exams.
